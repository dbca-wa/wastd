Privacy notice

This privacy statement sets out how we collect, use and disclose information collected through the Turtles Database website.

The Department of Biodiversity, Conservation and Attractions (DBCA) collects this personal information to have a record of:

  • Who processes marine turtles in the field.
  • Who reports stranded marine turtles in the field.

We may share this information with other organisations involved in turtle conservation to clarify data recorded, or, in the case of stranded turtles, seek the best animal welfare outcome.

The only information we are collecting is First Name, Last Name and email address.

If you choose not to provide first name, last name and email address, we will replace with the details of DBCA staff in charge of data collection or leave anonymous.

For further details on how DBCA manages your personal information, you can read our Privacy Policy. If you have any questions about how your personal information will be handled, or if you would like to access your personal information, please contact privacy@dbca.wa.gov.au.
